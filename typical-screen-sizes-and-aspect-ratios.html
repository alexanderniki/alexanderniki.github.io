Typical screen sizes and aspect ratios

Posted on Пн 20 Ноябрь 2017 in Design

Wide 16:9

  • 16:9 = 1.777
  • 2560:1440 = 1.777
  • 1920:1080 = 1.777
  • 1600:900 = 1.777
  • 1366:768 = 1.778
  • 1280:720 = 1.777
  • 960:540 = 1.777
  • 854:480 = 1.779
  • 1216:684 = 1.777 (android's navigation drawer)

Wide 16:10

  • 16:10 = 1.6
  • 2560:1600 = 1.6
  • 1920:1200 = 1.6
  • 1280:800 = 1.6
  • 800:480 = 1.666

Non-wide 4:3

  • 4:3 = 1.333
  • 2048:1536 = 1.333
  • 1600:1200 = 1333
  • 1024:768 = 1.333
  • 800:600 = 1.333

Square

  • 1:1 = 1
  • 720:720 = 1 (Blackberry Classic 3,5")
  • 1440:1440 = 1 (Blackberry Passport 4,5")
  • 1080:1080 = 1
