Posted on Вс 19 Ноябрь 2017 in Linux, HowTo

Here is a little note about how to determine CPU frequence in Linux.

Simple way

Open your terminal emulator and type:

$cat /proc/cpuinfo | grep MHz

After that, you'll see something like

cpu MHz     : 932.128
cpu MHz     : 1000.354
cpu MHz     : 998.950
